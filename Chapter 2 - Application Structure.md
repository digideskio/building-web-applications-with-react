# Chapter 2 - Application Structure

There is a quote, commonly attributed to Phil Karlton:

> There are two hard things in computer science: cache invalidation and naming things.

For those of us who sometime spend 15 minutes trying to find *just* the perfect name for that one method, naming may be excruciating. Deciding what to name things *and* where to place them may be akin to torture.

For that reason boilerplate and starter projects exist. Also, because everyone's got an opinion.

I belong to that group of people who spend inordinate amount of time on naming those methods and folders. So, to face my fears, I'll start from a discussion on the project structure.
